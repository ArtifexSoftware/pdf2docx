pdf2docx.shape.Paths module¶

Objects representing PDF path (both stroke and filling) extracted by page.getDrawings(). This method is new since PyMuPDF 1.18.0, with both pdf raw path and annotations like Line, Square and Highlight considered.

  • https://pymupdf.readthedocs.io/en/latest/page.html#Page.getDrawings

  • https://pymupdf.readthedocs.io/en/latest/faq.html#extracting-drawings
